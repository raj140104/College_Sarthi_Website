Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How can I contact the college administration?

A: You can reach the college administration through the Contact Us page, where you'll find contact details, a contact form, and department-specific information.

Q: What are the office hours for the Admissions Department?

A: The Admissions Department is open from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Please note that office hours may vary during holidays and breaks.

Q: Can I schedule a campus tour?

A: Yes, you can schedule a campus tour through the Contact Us page or by contacting our Admissions Department. We'd be happy to show you around!

Why College Sarthi ?


The College Sarthi offers a comprehensive array of features to enhance the college experience. From academic counseling to job placement, students have access to a wealth of support services. The platform's mobile app integration ensures easy access to resources and keeps students informed with push notifications. With a rich library of articles, blogs, and forums, it serves as a valuable resource hub for career advice and academic tips. The community forum and local business collaborations promote networking and community building. Additionally, the website offers conveniences like room rentals and classifieds, making daily college life more manageable. Overall, it's a one-stop solution for students seeking career guidance, academic resources, and community connections.
